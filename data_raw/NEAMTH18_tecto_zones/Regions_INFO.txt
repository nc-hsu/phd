File Regions_Tectonics_MwMax_Values_HDF5.txt format:
Column 1 | String | Delimiter ":" | IDHDF5 | NEAM region unique identifier
Column 2 | Integer | Delimiter "," | Tectonic_code | Code of the tectonic setting
Column 3 | Float | Delimiter "," | SHEEC_Mw | Maximum observed Mw from the SHEEC catalogue
Column 4 | Float | Delimiter "," | NOA_Mw | Maximum observed Mw from the NOA catalogue
Column 5 | Float | Delimiter "," | ISCGEM_Mw | Maximum observed Mw from the ISCGEM catalogue
Column 6 | Float | Delimiter "," | GCMT_Mw | Maximum observed Mw from the GCMT catalogue
Column 7 | Float | Delimiter "," | All_CAT | Maximum observed Mw from all catalogues (SHEEC, NOA, ISCGEM, GCMT)
Column 8 | Integer | Delimiter "," | SUPER_ZONE | Identifier of the SHARE superzone overlapping the region
Column 9 | Float | Delimiter "," | SHASZ_Mw | Maximum Mw from the overlapping SHARE superzone
Column 10 | Float | Delimiter "," | SHASZ_MwU | Maximum Mw from the overlapping SHARE superzone plus uncertainty
Column 11 | Float | Delimiter "," | EDSFSZ_Mw | Maximum Mw from the overlapping SHARE superzone inferred from EDSF
Column 12 | Float | Delimiter "," | All_SRC | Maximum Mw from all sources (catalogues and SHARE superzones)
Column 13 | Float | Delimiter "," | All_CAT_Sett | Maximum observed Mw in all tectonic settings from all catalogues
Column 14 | Float | Delimiter "," | All_SRC_Sett | Maximum Mw in all tectonic settings from all sources (catalogues and SHARE superzones)
Column 15 | Float | Delimiter "," | MmaxCrustOnly | Maximum Mw to be used for crustal regions only, obtained as: CEILING(If All_SRC < 6.5 Then MmaxToBeUsed = 6.5 Else MmaxToBeUsed = All_SRC End If; 0.1) 
Column 16 | Float | Delimiter "EOR" | MmaxCrustSubd | Maximum Mw to be used considering also the presence of subduction (Mmax = 9.00 according to Woessner et al., 2015, SHARE ESHM13, adopted also for the Caribbean, and the Cadiz region treated as SpecialBS)

File Regions_Tectonics_BSPS_HDF5.txt format: (BS-PS separation)
Column 1 | String | Delimiter ":" | ID | Region unique identifier
Column 2 | Integer | Delimiter "EOR" | BSPS | Code 0-5 with the following meanings:
0: Nothing
1: BS only (Gaussians)
2: PS Crustal only 
3: PS Subduction only (Caribbean)
4: BS+PS Crustal
5: BS+PS Subduction (Mediterranean)
